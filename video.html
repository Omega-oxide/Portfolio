Video Script/Demo

The final project was to create a video showing users how to operate a the given subject. So my video is a demo of the game Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain. First was the script for it and then the video itself.

Script:

The script is a basic outline of what I wanted to show in the video. A hypothetical ideal situation for each of my given ideas for different aporaches to the mission. The final video is very different but the best parts of the script are still in the video.

Video:

The video shows the user how to sucessfully complete Mission 8 in the game. It demos multiple game mechanics and how to use them to complete the mission. This was the first time I have used video editing software and I learned a lot.

This video assignment's goal is to show how diverse the games mechanics are and how to use them. Each section tries to capitalize on a number of different mechanics. It is a demo and guide to Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain since its launch back in 2015 on PS3, PS4, Xbox 360, Xbox One, and PC.
