Textbook Guide on Managing Mother Base

The third assignment was to create a textbook excert on MGSV. This is a little different from the other two assignments since it is a textbook. It follows a two column format with a few pictures and alot of text sectioned off. The chapter entry is on managing Mother Base in the game. Its an entry level guide to running most of the Mother Base functions, but explains the end game capabilities of the base.

The goal of this textbook entry is to show the reader how to manage Mother Base, how to build it up, and how to use the resources it provides. It is a guide to Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain since its launch back in 2015 on PS3, PS4, Xbox 360, Xbox One, and PC.
