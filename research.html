Researching MGSV

Researching information on the game was my first assignment. I used it's Wikipedia entry as a basis on what my headings should be and where I should go research. The Fandom Wiki was also very helpful to discribe found information that the comunity cobbled together. Many acclaimed video game review and news outlets exstensively went over many dev facts, game content, reception, and other useful information to build my report.

The goal of this report is to show the reader the game, its content, its development, and the reception it got to fill the reader in on what this game is. It is a summary of information on Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain since its launch back in 2015 on PS3, PS4, Xbox 360, Xbox One, and PC.
